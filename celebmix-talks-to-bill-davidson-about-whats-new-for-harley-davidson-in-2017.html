    Harley Davidson is a name and a brand that everyone is familiar with, motorcycle fanatic or not.  A Harley isn’t just a motorcycle, it’s a symbol of freedom and being unafraid to embrace the unknown. Founded in 1903, the company has come to embrace the human instinct to be free, and what better way than riding down an open road feeling the earth all around you.

    Everyone from your neighbor to your favorite celebrity rides a Harley, and when looking at bikes on the road, they’re the brand most frequently seen.  When a company puts as much time, effort, and passion into their bikes as Harley Davidson does, their product stands the test of time.

    As the years have gone by, the motorcycle has certainly shown differences in look, speed, and performance, but the heart of the Harley has never changed. In 2017, the touring motorcycle has been reconstructed to provide an entirely new ride.

    We talked to Bill Davidson to discuss what riders can expect from the 2017 touring motorcycles, what other products you should be on the look out for, and the Milwaukee Motorcycle Rally & Bike Show.

    Bill’s passion for the 2017 motor and what it does for Harley Davidson is evident in our conversation and we have to admit, we’re jealous of everyone who’s been able to test out the motorcycle already.

    To find out how you can test drive the latest model, or any other that suits you, visit Harley Davidson‘s website – you’ll also find out more about the story behind the brand, the sense of community that comes with owning a Harley, and the countless options available to make your motorcycle totally one of a kind.
