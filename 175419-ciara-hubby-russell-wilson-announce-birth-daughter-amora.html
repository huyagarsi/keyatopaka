    American singer Ciara and her husband, Russell Wilson, have announced the birth of their daughter.

    How did Ciara announce pregnancy?

    In August, the 37-year-old singer joyfully disclosed her pregnancy, anticipating her fourth child and third with her hubby Russell.

    The couple chose to share their exhilarating baby news through an Instagram video announcement, which, according to PEOPLE, was artfully filmed by the NFL star during their anniversary escapade to Japan in July.
