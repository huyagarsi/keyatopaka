    Jack Stirrup is the character that no one has an accurate read on. One of the deckhands on the fourth season of Below Deck Mediterranean, Jack had his moments on the show. Even though he was not recalled for the fifth season of the show, he was quite the character in the fourth season. His wavy locks and thick Liverpool accent made him quite popular amongst the ladies. He even started a relationship with the 2nd stewardess Aesha, but that was neither here nor there. In this article, we will take a closer look at the wiki-bio of Jack Stirrup.

    Wiki/Bio Facts

    Early Life

    Jack Stirrup was born and raised in Liverpool. Little is known about Jack’s early life and the man is in no hurry to change it. Before making out as a yachtie, Jack worked as an engineer in the Merchant Navy. On the deck, Jack often joked about his life on the navy and shared his shenanigans. In one of the episodes, Jack revealed about a time when he shaved one of his legs to imagine that a woman was sitting beside him.
    During his time in the Merchant Navy, Jack spent most of his time in the French Riviera at bars. As a result, he lost a ton of money and had to look for work in a boat. This eventually brought jack into yachting and Below deck.

    Yachting

    Even though Jack was working here and there, things were not exactly on the rise. Things changed for him when he was brought on the fourth season of the TV series, Below Deck Mediterranean. Even though he hated working and avoided work at all costs, he got things done when he had to. A free-spirited guy, Jack was often reminded by his fellow cast members to wear shoes while leaving the boat. This often led to hilarious situations during the team’s outing.

    Relationship on board

    The ever comic character, Jack was perfect for the fun-loving second stew, Aesha. The pair enjoyed flirtatious moments on board and were all but set for a potential relationship. However, things were never meant to be for the pair. In particular, Aesha was irked by Jack’s comments to Hannah during a group dinner.
    Hannah who seemed to be feeling under the weather seemed upset during the dinner, to which Jack commented,
    “why is your face, looks like you’ve just been raped?”

    Aesha called Jack out on the comment but Jack denied ever saying so. But when Aesha opened up about being the survivor of sexual assault, Jack was quick to apologize. In an interview with Bravo TV, Jack commented on the episode.

    “Just thinking about the fact that I had joked about something could have brought that person back to that situation and made them think about that horrible situation”.

    Aesha looked over the whole situation and forgave Jack.

    Life After Below Deck

    Despite his difficulties, Jack managed to finish the season and left the yacht with Aesha. He invited her to come to London and meet his family, but he also had a secret. He was still in love with his ex-girlfriend Kelly Hidge, who he had dated before joining the show. He decided to break up with Aesha and go back to Kelly, leaving Aesha heartbroken and confused.

    Jack and Kelly soon had some happy news. They were expecting their first child together, a son named Phoenix Sidney Stirrup. Jack announced the birth of his baby boy on social media in April 2020. He expressed his love and gratitude for his son and his girlfriend.

    He has not revealed what he is doing for work now, but he may have left the yachting industry for good. He may have returned to his previous career as a Merchant Navy engineer or pursued something else entirely. In the meantime, Jack’s relationship status with Kelly is also unclear as of now.

    Net Worth

    Jack’s net worth and sources of income are not publicly known, but he may have earned some money from his appearance on Below Deck Mediterranean season 4. He worked as a deckhand on the Sirocco yacht. He may have also made some income from his previous career as a Merchant Navy engineer.
