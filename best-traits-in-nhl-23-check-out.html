    Best Traits In NHL 23 Check Out Full Details

    In whatever game style in NHL 23, there are a variety of options for you to edge out the competition. Using some of the various traits accessible is one of the finest methods to acquire a tiny advantage over the competition.

    23 Jun 2024, 00:00

    In whatever game style in NHL 23, there are a variety of options for you to edge out the competition. Using some of the various traits accessible is one of the finest methods to acquire a tiny advantage over the competition.

    The greatest traits from NHL 23 can be used to boost your strengths or decrease your weaknesses when building your own player, even if players in the game already have their traits allocated to them.

    But what qualities in NHL 23 stand out the most?

    There are 34 attributes available for selection, but there are only two spots, so you must be selective. This post will list the qualities you should choose for your pro in NHL 23.

    Unlock Player Traits in NHL 23

    You’ll observe that you can only use the Zone ability and a limited number of basic boosts when your first campaign begins.

    You must play the game to gain experience and advance through the stages in order to unlock new boosters and the top X-factor abilities.

    You will get a few boosts and, frequently, a Superstar ability each time you accumulate enough experience to level up your character.

    Jump into the ProAm chapters to gain several qualities and skills if you want to advance as rapidly as possible.

    Player Traits in NHL 23

    NHL 23 offers 34 player attributes to choose from. You can’t just use any of these attributes, it’s crucial to remember that. You need to already possess certain qualities.

    To add your preferred features to your player, you can modify your NHL 23 attributes to suit their requirements.

    Best Power Traits

    Steady Hands (+2 Puck Control)

    Puck control is a necessary statistic in addition to speed and shot power if you want to be a great NHL player.

    The ice can appear incredibly congested at times, so having exceptional puck control is crucial to making sure your player doesn’t lose possession all the time. The Steady Hands trait, which grants +2 Puck Control, calls for 74 balance.

    Steady Slapper (+2 Slapshot Power)

    Consider adding the Steady Slapper characteristic to your repertoire if you want to increase the potency of your slapshot. It will double your slapshot power but calls for a balance statistic of 72.

    Steady Wrister (+2 Wrist Shot Power)

    As we’ve already mentioned, mastering the wrist shot in NHL 23 is a fantastic method to boost your offense.

    The Steady Wrister characteristic gives your player a bonus +2 wrist shot power, allowing you to attempt this type of shot from a little further away or use it more successfully in regions where it is more common.

    You need a balancing score of 72 in order to unlock this characteristic.

    Best Tactics Traits

    Controlled Deking (+4 Deking)

    The Controlled Deking trait is a fantastic choice if you want to give your player extra deking power but your agility is under 80.

    You receive the same deking improvements, but your agility score isn’t as high. You must have a puck control score of at least 82.

    This is How to Win Faceoffs in NHL 23

    Agile Deking (+4 Deking)

    It can be discouraging to keep attempting to pass your way around strong opposing defenders only to fail time and time again.

    Deke maneuvers can help you get through well-constructed defenses and spice up your play.

    If your agility is 80 or higher, the Agile Deking trait will improve your deking by 4.

    Best Playstyle Traits

    Offensive Threat (+4 Offensive Awareness)

    One of the crucial metrics in NHL 23 is offensive awareness, which is one that many people use to identify the top young players in the game.

    Your offensive awareness will improve by four if you have the Offensive Threat trait, but your deking score must be at least 80 to qualify.

    Discipline IQ (+4 Defensive Awareness)

    While it’s important to make sure your player can contribute offensively to your squad, you also need to make sure he can play solid defense.

    As long as your discipline is at 78, you can increase your defensive awareness by 4 with the Discipline IQ attribute.

    Discipline Stick (+4 Stick Checking)

    Discipline Stick is a feature you might want to think about if your discipline score is on or above 78. This skill raises stick-checking by four and elevates your player to the next level, giving your squad a new dimension.

    Best Tenacity Traits

    Tenacious Shield (+4 Shot Blocking)

    Last but not least, the Tenacious Shield is a fantastic choice for you if you would want to use one of your trait spots to increase your shot-blocking because it provides +4 shot-blocking.

    You may become a team’s best defender with this quality. Just be certain that it is 78 or higher.

    Power Fighter (+4 Fighting Skill)

    Without one feature that helps you become a better fighter, this list of the best traits would fall short. You will have a 4 percent fighting ability boost thanks to the Power Fighter trait, which will raise your chances of winning battles in NHL 23.

    Even though it is not a necessary ability to win games in NHL 23, learning how to fight is nonetheless crucial. But it’s a lot of fun!

    Stable Steel (+4 Durability)

    There are various fascinating tenacious attributes for you to consider if you decide to concentrate on other aspects of your athlete rather than his attacking, speed, or shooting talents.

    As long as your balancing score is 75 or higher, the Stable Steel trait helps increase your durability by 4.

    Strong Steel (+4 Durability)

    Consider the Strong Steel trait if you want to boost your durability by 4 but don’t want to give up skill points to raise your balance to 75 or higher.

    The durability benefits from this characteristic are the same, but you’ll require a strength score of 78 rather than a balance score of 75 or higher.

    Best Technique Traits

    Precise Wrister (+2 Wrist Shot Accuracy)

    The wrist shot is one of the most crucial offensive options you have in NHL 23, as you are surely already aware.

    It is an excellent weapon for shooting from a distance or when numerous defenders are surrounding you.

    A excellent approach to increase the number of goals you score with this deft skill shot is to increase your wrist shot accuracy with the Precise Wrister trait. To unlock this characteristic, you need an Offensive Awareness statistic of 80.

    Precise Slapper (+2 Slap Shot Accuracy)

    The slap shot, which includes producing a lot of power and smacking the ball toward the goal, is another effective shooting style in NHL 23.

    Although many players have strong slap shots, they frequently go wayward or are hit-and-miss. The Precise Slapper trait will help you rapidly enhance your slap shot. Just keep in mind that to unlock it, you must have 80 overall awareness.

    Power Skating (+2 Speed)

    Any great player in NHL 23 needs to possess excellent shooting skills as well as quickness. Your player will be able to sprint past the opposition and make room for a shot. For the Power Skating trait to be unlocked, you need 78 strength.

    Final Message

    What playstyle you prefer will ultimately determine which attributes you use in World of CHEL or other online battles.  You should put your efforts into making a player that fits your playstyle and structure and make use of characteristics that will facilitate your success. Before deciding which traits to add to your player, consider the playstyle you wish to employ.
