    Name:Florence Henderson

    Hometown:Dale, IN

    Birthday:February 14, 1934

    About

    Florence Henderson, best known for her role as Carol Brady in the ‘70s sitcom, The Brady Bunch, was born in 1934 in Dale, Indiana. 

    Following the role that made her a TV icon, Henderson has done small things here and there, such as co-hosting the daily talk show Living Live! with Meshach Taylor on Retirement Living TV.

    Since 2008, the famous TV mom has been the host of her own television series on RLTV, called The Florence Henderson Show. She also competed on the eleventh season of Dancing With the Stars. 

    In February 2013, Henderson began hosting her own cooking show, Who’s Cooking with Florence Henderson.

    The actress married Ira Bernstein in January 1956 and the couple had four children. During her marriage, she has acknowledged infidelity, including an affair with former New York City mayor, John Lindsay. In 1985, Bernstein and Henderson divorced.

    She later married hypnotherapist John Kappas in 1987, who passed away in 2002.
