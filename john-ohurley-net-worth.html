    Early Life

    John O’Hurley, born John George O’Hurley Jr., came into the world on October 9, 1954, in the town of Kittery, Maine. His father, John Sr., worked as an ear, nose, and throat surgeon, while his mother, Jean, took care of their home as a housewife.

    During his early education, O’Hurley attended Natick High School in Massachusetts and later Kingswood-Oxford School in Connecticut.
