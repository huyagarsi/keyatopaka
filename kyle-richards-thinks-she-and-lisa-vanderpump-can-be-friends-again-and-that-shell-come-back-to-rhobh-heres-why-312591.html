    25 May 2024, 00:00

    Kyle Richard believes it’s possible that if she and Lisa Vanderpump make peace, the latter could actually return to the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills as well! Fans know that LVP exited the show, meaning that at this point, Kyle is the only OG cast member left but she still has hope, it seems.

    It sounds like Kyle has been looking back at her past on RHOBH, including her former friendship with Lisa and she might just miss the good ol’ times!

    Back in April, Lisa made the official announcement that she would no longer be part of the cast in the new season!

    Part of what led to her exit was the falling out with Kyle Richards that was all documented by the Bravo cameras.

    But despite their ugly feud, Kyle is now hopeful that they can be friends once again.

    This is what a source tells HollywoodLife!

    Allegedly, ‘There’s a small piece of Kyle that is sad she is to begin another season and not have Lisa there since they did start the show together, but Kyle feels content with where she is at with LVP versus where they were earlier this year. Kyle feels the time’s healed some of the problems and the pain for her. She just thinks that Lisa blew this so far out of proportion, but at the end of the day, she does wish her well.’

    ‘Deep down, Kyle has confidence that she and Lisa can be friends again and that she would even return to the show, but Kyle’s really happy to be doing it, with or without Lisa. Lisa’s suffered so much loss this past year and is doing her best to cope.’
