    Uriah Shelton Career

    Without a Trace, The Ghost Whisperer, Mad TV, Dirty Sexy Money, The Suite Life on Deck, Monk, Trust Me, Trauma, Hallmark’s The Nanny Express, and the independent feature Alabama Moon are among the television shows in which Shelton has appeared.

    In the film Lifted, Shelton played the lead character Henry Matthews. In the film, Shelton wrote and performed the song “I Miss You.” He appeared as Joshua Matthews on the Disney Channel’s Girl Meets World, a spin-off of the 1990s sitcom Boy Meets World. Shelton was also seen in the A&E drama The Glades (2010-2013). He portrayed Jeff Cargill, the son of Kiele Sanchez’s nurse.

    In 2020, he portrayed the love interest of Kathryn Newton’s lead character in the well-reviewed horror comedy film Freaky.
