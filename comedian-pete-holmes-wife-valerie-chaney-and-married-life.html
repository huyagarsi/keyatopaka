    Pete Holmes wife Valerie Chaney is a fitness coach and trainer. Valerie and Pete got married on 22nd October 2017.

    Holmes is an American comedian, actor, and podcaster. He is best known for his appearance in the HBO television series Crashing where he appeared in 24 episodes between 2017 and 2019.

    He debuted in the 2007 television series Honesty and has since amassed 33 acting credits on his IMDb profile.

    He also hosted The Pete Holmes Show for nearly a year between October 28, 2013, and June 18, 2014. The show aired two seasons and 80 episodes in its entire run. The show followed a one-hour format and aired Monday through Thursday at midnight on TBS.

    However, Pete gained recognition in the early 2010s with his standup comedies. He also launched his podcast series  You Made It Weird in 2011 and released four comedy specials between 2011 and 2018.

    Valerie Chaney Is A Fitness Guru

    Pete Holmes wife Valerie Chaney is a fitness coach and dance instructor. Valerie offers meditation and fitness courses around California in different studios.

    According to Valerie’s Instagram post, she is most recently teaching dancing at Starborn Wellness in Ojai, California. She instructs in non-competitive dancing to stay fit and healthy. Valerie has urged her followers to join her at the studio on Fridays at 10 am.

    She has also worked with The Beat House in Atwater Village, California, where she provided non-competitive dance lessons to her clients through physical as well as virtual mediums. Valerie also served her clients with on-demand lessons.

    Valerie also conducted a day-long virtual yoga retreat titled Rest in Simplicity Cultivate Joy. The event was held on November 27, 2022, from 9.30 in the morning to 4.30 in the evening. People could enjoy the retreat for a full day for just $10, according to Pete’s Instagram post.

    She also conducted another online event titled Being Here: An Introduction to Mindfulness where she discussed ways of cultivating compassion and joy by practicing mindfulness. Valerie hosts her online events through Event Brite.

    Valerie is found on Instagram with her username @valerieandchaney. She has amassed nearly 11k followers and has shared 655 posts on her picture-sharing social media account.

    Unfortunately, their relationship did not continue long and the pair called quits just after 6 years of being married. Pete’s HBO show Crashing is a loose adaptation of his real-life love story and his divorce, according to Inverse.
