    Kim Petras releases new single 'Blow It All'

    Kim Petras is the gift that keeps on giving. Hot on the heels of her recent releases Broken and Got My Number, Kim is cementing her new era in style with another new single called Blow It All.

    28 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Kim Petras is the gift that keeps on giving.

    Hot on the heels of her recent releases ‘Broken’ and ‘Got My Number’, Kim is cementing her new era in style with another new single called ‘Blow It All’.

    Armed with hip-hop undertones, ‘Blow It All’ is a slick pop-bop with a chilled vibe, the perfect soundtrack to those long summer nights. The track sees Kim living her best life and making the most of enjoying herself while she can, because one day it will be too late.

    Feel like I made it, waited all my life
    Everybody rollin’ tryna feel alive
    Can’t take it with ya, blow it all tonight
    All tonight, blow it all tonight

    Check out the lyric video for ‘Blow It All’ below.

    ‘Blow It All’ is the third release from Kim in as many weeks, marking the beginning of her new era. Whilst her trademark pop stamp is still evident, it’s clear to see that Kim has been busy honing a luscious new personal sound which intertwines trap beats and hip-hop.

    Kim will be heading out on a huge US tour this summer, and will also be performing at UK festivals including Reading, Leeds and Manchester Pride.
